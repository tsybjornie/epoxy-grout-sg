FAQ

Can professional grout cleaning fix stained grout?

Professional cleaning helps temporarily, but stains in cement grout are absorbed deep into the material. Within weeks to months, stains return. Epoxy regrouting is the permanent fix.

Absolutely! Kitchen grout staining from cooking oil is extremely common in Singapore. We remove the old stained grout and replace with oil-resistant epoxy that wipes clean easily.
