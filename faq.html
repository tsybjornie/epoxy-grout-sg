Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our epoxy grout services

Service & Process

What is epoxy grout and how is it different from cement grout?
Epoxy grout is a two-part resin-based grout that is 100% waterproof, non-porous, and highly resistant to stains, mold, and bacteria. Unlike traditional cement grout which absorbs water and discolours over time, epoxy grout maintains its colour and integrity for 15+ years. It's ideal for Singapore and JB's humid climate.
How long does the application process take?
Most bathroom or kitchen projects are completed within 1 working day. Full home packages (kitchen + 2 bathrooms) typically take 1–2 days depending on the total area and complexity. We'll confirm the exact timeline during the free site inspection.
What brands of epoxy grout do you use?
We use premium professional-grade epoxy grout from trusted brands including Ardex, Sika, Mapei Kerapoxy, and Laticrete. The specific brand may vary based on availability, tile type, and joint width. All brands we use are industry-leading and come with manufacturer warranties.
Do you remove the old grout before applying epoxy?
Yes. Our standard process includes removing existing grout, thorough cleaning and preparation of the tile joints, application of epoxy grout, and final cleanup. Proper removal is essential for adhesion and longevity of the new epoxy grout.
Can I choose my grout colour?
Absolutely. We offer 40+ colours across all our brand partners. You can preview colours on our website, and we provide physical sample swatches for approval before beginning any work. Custom colour matching is also available on request.

Curing & Aftercare

How long does epoxy grout take to cure / dry?
Epoxy grout typically requires 24–48 hours to fully cure. During this curing period, the treated area must NOT be walked on, used, or exposed to water. The exact curing time depends on the brand used, temperature, humidity, and joint depth. We will advise you on the specific curing time after application.
What happens if I walk on the grout before it's fully cured?
Walking on, using, or allowing water contact with uncured epoxy grout can cause permanent damage including cracking, discolouration, uneven surfaces, or delamination. Any damage caused by premature use of the treated area during the curing period is the client's responsibility and is not covered under our warranty or eligible for touch-ups, refunds, or rework. We strongly advise all household members, tenants, and visitors to stay away from treated areas until the curing period has elapsed.
How should I maintain epoxy grout after curing?
Epoxy grout is very low maintenance. Simply clean with a mild detergent and soft cloth or mop. Avoid abrasive cleaners, steel wool, or harsh chemicals (e.g. bleach-based products) on the grout lines. For best results, wipe up spills promptly and avoid dragging heavy or sharp objects across the grouted surface.
Can I enter the room while the grout is drying?
No. We strongly advise against entering, using, or allowing any foot traffic in the treated area during the 24–48 hour curing window. This includes pets and children. If you need access to other parts of your home, please plan accordingly before the service date. We are not liable for any damage resulting from premature access to the treated area.
