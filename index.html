Common Grout Problems We Solve

Singapore's humidity is grout's worst enemy — here's what goes wrong and how we fix it

—

Black Mold & Mildew

Porous cement grout absorbs moisture in SG/JB's 80%+ humidity, becoming a breeding ground for harmful mold and bacteria.

Solution: Non-porous epoxy grout blocks 100% moisture — zero mold growth
—

Discoloration & Staining

Curry, soy sauce, coffee — cement grout absorbs everything. Yellow/brown stained grout lines make your tiles look old.

Solution: Epoxy grout is stain-proof — wipe clean, stays bright forever
—

Cracking & Crumbling

Heat expansion + poor installation cause grout to crack and fall out. Water then seeps behind tiles causing damage.

Solution: Epoxy grout has 3x the flexural strength — no cracking
—

Efflorescence (White Powder)

White salt deposits appear when moisture travels through cement grout. Indicates hidden water damage underneath.

Solution: Epoxy grout = zero water migration. Problem eliminated.
—

Colour Mismatch & Fading

Cement/powder grout colour varies with mixing ratio, drying speed, and batch differences. Looks patchy within months.

Solution: Epoxy grout delivers perfectly uniform colour that never fades
—

Water Seepage & Leaks

Failed grout lets water penetrate behind tiles, causing structural damage, peeling, and costly repairs.

Solution: Epoxy grout creates a waterproof barrier. Tiles stay sealed.

Why You Can't DIY Epoxy Grout

Epoxy grout is NOT like regular grout — here's why professionals exist for this

—

10-Minute Working Time

Epoxy grout hardens FAST. Once mixed, you have only 10-15 minutes before it becomes unworkable. Professionals work in small batches with practiced speed. DIYers waste $50-80 of material per failed batch.

Our crew mixes, applies, and cleans in a choreographed workflow — zero waste
—

Precise 2-Part Chemical Mixing

Epoxy grout requires exact resin-to-hardener ratios. Wrong mix = won't cure, stays sticky forever, or becomes brittle and cracks. There's no fixing a bad mix — you have to remove and start over.

We use calibrated mixing for Grade 1 epoxy — the best flexibility & UV resistance
—

Haze Removal Nightmare

Epoxy leaves a stubborn haze on tiles that's nearly impossible to remove once dried. Store-bought DIY kits can't be scrubbed properly — making daily cleaning impractical. Professionals use specialised solvents and timing.

We clean as we go with professional-grade haze removers — your tiles stay pristine
—

Specialised Tools Required

Proper application needs commercial grout floats, margin trowels, sponges, and sometimes oscillating multi-tools for removal. Store-bought kits include cheap tools that leave poor results and uneven lines.

We bring $2,000+ worth of professional tools to every job
—

Colour Consistency is Impossible

DIY mixing creates colour variations between batches. Different humidity, temperature, and mixing ratios all affect the final colour. One bathroom can end up with 3 different shades of "white."

We achieve 99% colour match using machine mixing — same colour even years later
—

Mistakes Cost MORE Than Hiring Us

Failed DIY epoxy grout requires professional removal + redo. Average DIY failure cost: $500-1,200 in wasted materials, tools, and eventual professional cleanup. You save money by hiring us first.

Our service + materials averages $4.80/sqft — cheaper than one DIY mistake

7 out of 10 DIY epoxy grout attempts end in failure

We regularly get called to fix DIY jobs gone wrong. The epoxy didn't cure, the haze won't come off, the colour is patchy, or tiles got damaged during removal of the failed attempt. Save yourself the headache — let the professionals handle it from the start.

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know before booking

How long does the service take? ▼

Most single-area jobs (1 bathroom or kitchen) are completed in 1 day. Full home service takes 2-3 days depending on size. For a 4-room HDB: bathroom = 1 day, kitchen = 1-2 days, living+dining = 2 days. You can stay at home during service — just avoid the working area while grout cures.

Is there any smell or mess? ▼

No odour and minimal mess! Unlike old-school cementitious grout, premium epoxy grout is VOC-free and odourless. We lay protective sheets and clean up completely after each session. Safe for babies, pets, and elderly — no need to vacate your home.

Can epoxy grout be applied over existing grout? ▼

Yes! If your existing grout is structurally sound (not crumbling or deeply cracked), we can apply epoxy grout directly over it — this is our "New Application" service from $4.80/sqft. If your grout has mold, major cracks, or is falling apart, we recommend full removal + replacement from $6.80/sqft for best results.

What brands do you use? ▼

We only use Grade 1 premium brands: Ardex EG 15/18 (Germany), Sika/Davco (SG/MY), Mapei Kerapoxy CQ (Italy), and Laticrete SpectraLock (USA). Grade 1 epoxy has the best flexibility, UV resistance, and durability — essential for Singapore's extreme humidity and heat. Many competitors use cheaper Grade 3-5 materials that become brittle over time.

How long does epoxy grout last? ▼

Premium epoxy grout lasts 15-20+ years with zero maintenance. No re-sealing needed, ever. Compare that to cementitious grout which typically needs replacement every 5-8 years, plus annual re-sealing. Epoxy grout is truly a "do it once, forget it forever" solution.

Do you serve HDB, condo, and landed properties? ▼

Yes! We serve all property types — HDB (BTO & resale), condos, landed homes, and commercial spaces. We also serve Johor Bahru, Malaysia with JB-friendly pricing (extra 10% off). Whether it's a 2-room BTO or a bungalow, we've got you covered.

When can the service be done? ▼

Service hours: Monday-Saturday, 9am-5pm. You can book whether you're living in or planning renovation. Before renovation: after defect handover. After renovation: after post-renovation deep cleaning. WhatsApp us to check slot availability — slots fill up fast.

What's the difference between your service and cheap grout pens? ▼

Grout pens and DIY "grout paint" are cosmetic — they coat the surface but don't replace the grout. They peel off within weeks, can't withstand scrubbing, and provide zero waterproofing. Our service applies actual 2-part epoxy grout that chemically bonds to your tiles, creating a permanent, waterproof, stain-proof seal that lasts decades.
