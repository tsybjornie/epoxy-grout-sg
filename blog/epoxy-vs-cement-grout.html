Epoxy Grout vs Cement Grout: Which Is Better for Singapore?

By CLEANGROUT | Updated February 2026 | 5 min read

If you are regrouting your Singapore home, you have two main options: cement grout (the traditional choice) and epoxy grout (the modern upgrade). This guide breaks down every difference to help you decide.

The Verdict: Epoxy Wins for Singapore

In Singapore's 80-90% humidity, cement grout is fighting a losing battle. It absorbs moisture, grows mold, and stains permanently within 2-3 years. Epoxy grout costs more upfront but lasts 3-4x longer and stays looking brand new.

Cost per year: Cement grout at lasting 5 years = /year. Epoxy grout at lasting 15 years = /year. Epoxy is actually cheaper long-term.

When Cement Grout Still Makes Sense

  • Very tight budget and DIY installation
  • Outdoor areas with very wide joints (>10mm)
  • Temporary solution before renovation

When to Choose Epoxy Grout

  • Any bathroom or wet area
  • Kitchen backsplash and floor
  • Properties you want to sell (increases value)
  • Anyone tired of cleaning moldy grout every month
