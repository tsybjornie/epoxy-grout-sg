How to Maintain Epoxy Grout | Cleaning Guide Singapore

By CLEANGROUT | Updated February 2026 | 5 min read

Good news: epoxy grout is incredibly low-maintenance. Unlike cement grout that needs constant scrubbing, epoxy grout stays clean with minimal effort. Here is your complete care guide.

Weekly Cleaning (2 Minutes)

  1. Wipe grout lines with a damp cloth or sponge
  2. Use any mild household cleaner (dishwashing liquid works fine)
  3. Rinse with clean water
  4. That is it - no scrubbing needed

Monthly Deep Clean (5 Minutes)

  1. Mix warm water with a tablespoon of baking soda
  2. Apply to grout lines with a soft cloth
  3. Let sit for 5 minutes
  4. Wipe clean with damp cloth

What NOT to Use on Epoxy Grout

AvoidWhyUse Instead
BleachCan discolor over timeMild soap
Abrasive scrubbersMay scratch surfaceSoft cloth/sponge
Vinegar (undiluted)Acidic - may dull finishDiluted solution
Steel woolScratches permanentlyMicrofiber cloth

Singapore-Specific Tips

  • Ventilation: Keep bathroom fan running for 15 minutes after showering to reduce moisture buildup on tiles
  • Squeegee: A quick squeegee after showering keeps everything spotless
  • Kitchen grease: Wipe cooking oil splashes immediately - epoxy grout resists stains but prevention is easier than cleaning
